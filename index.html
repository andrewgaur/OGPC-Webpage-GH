Our Seasons

Season 15

Architechs first began in 2021 at St. Pius X Middle School. Two of our current team leaders competed on the original team that year, winning Best in Show in Season 15. That year, Architechs created Paracide, a two-dimensional maze game depicting symbiotic relations between mutated alien parasites and human scientists.

Season 18

Architechs returned for the 2024/25 season at Jesuit High School, featuring mostly rookies and two returning members. That year, our team competed in the high school category for the first time, winning the Theme and Story award for our game, Cyclogenesis, a top-down civilization simulator, but aiming to destroy society rather than develop it.
